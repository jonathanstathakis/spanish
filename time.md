# Time

## Saying the Time

one in the afternoon: *uno de la tarde*

Time units are described as belonging to their supercategory: i.e. *de la manaña*, *de la tarde*, *de la noche*.

- *manaña* is used for 0000 - 1300
- *tarde* for 1300 - 1800
- *noche* for 1800 - 0000
