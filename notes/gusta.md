Intransitive verb.

Examples of usage:

"A Eduardo le gusta" = Eduardo likes

"Nos gusta" = we like

To talk about what multiple people like: "Les gusta"

i.e. "Les gusta ir al parque" = They like going to the park

"a mi me gusta.." I like "A ti te gusta.." "a ellos les gusta.."
